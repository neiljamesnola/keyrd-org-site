Your privacy is foundational to our mission. KeyRD collects only the information essential for delivering personalized, evidence-based nutrition nudges—with your explicit, revocable consent.

We are not a HIPAA-covered entity, but we voluntarily adhere to HIPAA-aligned principles: encryption in transit and at rest, strict access controls, and a transparent user rights policy.

We never sell or share your data. Anonymized usage patterns may be used to refine our recommendation engine and improve system performance.

You may request full deletion of your data at any time by emailing us at privacy@keyrd.org.

This policy will evolve as KeyRD matures. When it does, you’ll always be notified transparently and given meaningful choices about your data.
