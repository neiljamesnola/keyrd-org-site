Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 4, 2025

Your privacy is foundational to our mission. KeyRD collects only the information essential for delivering personalized, evidence-based nutrition nudges—always with your explicit, revocable consent.

We are not a HIPAA-covered entity, but we voluntarily adhere to HIPAA-aligned standards: encrypted data in transit and at rest, strict access control, and a transparent user rights policy.

We never sell or share your personal data. Anonymized patterns may be used internally to improve our algorithms and system performance.

You can request deletion of your data at any time by emailing privacy@keyrd.org.

This policy may evolve as we grow. If it does, we’ll notify you clearly and give you meaningful choices about your data.

© 2025 KeyRD. Privacy is not a feature — it’s the foundation.
