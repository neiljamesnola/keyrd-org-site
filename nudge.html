What is a Nudge?

Home Join Privacy Terms Contact Nudge

A nudge is a small, well-timed prompt that helps guide your behavior—without restricting your freedom of choice.

At KeyRD, our nudges are designed to be:

  • 🧠 Personalized: Tailored to your unique biometric patterns, lifestyle, and clinical needs.
  • ⏱️ Just-in-Time: Sent at the moments they matter most—before a meal, during stress, or post-activity.
  • 📚 Evidence-Based: Grounded in proven principles from behavioral science, nutrition, and clinical research.
  • 👐 Non-Coercive: Designed to assist, never insist. Nudges inform your decision—they don’t dictate it.

Unlike rigid diet plans or endless food tracking, nudges meet you where you are—helping shift behaviors gently but meaningfully over time.
